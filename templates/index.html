Our Mission

Carbon-X is a transparent, blockchain-inspired carbon credit ecosystem where industrial emitters can offset their footprint by purchasing verified credits from landowners who practice regenerative agriculture, reforestation, and conservation. We aim to make carbon trading accessible, auditable, and impactful.

🏭

1. Report Emissions

Companies log their CO₂ emissions through verified methods. Credits are allocated based on reduction targets.

🌾

2. Generate Credits

Landowners earn carbon credits by sequestering carbon via reforestation, soil management, or conservation projects.

🔄

3. Trade & Offset

Companies buy credits on the marketplace. Each transaction is immutable, transparent, and retired upon use.
